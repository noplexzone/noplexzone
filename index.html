FAQ & troubleshooting

Before you ask if it is down

Most problems are either temporary maintenance, a missing permission, a Plex sign-in mismatch, or a request still working through the download queue.

I cannot sign in to a tool.

Make sure you are using the same Plex account tied to your invite. Some services also require separate access to be enabled.

A service is not loading.

Use the UptimeRobot status page once it is linked here. If a requested item is missing, check Monitarr to see whether it is still queued or downloading.

Can I request access from here?

No public access form is available right now. Ask the person who referred you for a current invite link.

Do donations grant access?

No. Donations are optional and appreciated, but they do not guarantee access, priority, or special treatment.
